Privacy Policy

This policy explains how StepGate handles your data.

Last updated: 2026-03-31

Introduction

StepGate is a mobile app that helps you limit access to selected apps and regain access through movement. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is used.

What data StepGate accesses

  • App usage data such as usage duration and selected apps.
  • Steps / activity data to calculate your daily goal and adaptive access.

Permissions used

  • Usage Access — read app usage for selected apps.
  • Accessibility Service — enforce app blocking when limits are reached.
  • Activity Recognition / Health Connect — read step data.
  • Overlay permission — show the block screen.

How the data is used

  • Calculate your baseline time from recent usage history.
  • Set your daily step goal based on your typical activity.
  • Determine available time for controlled apps each day.
  • Enforce blocking when the daily access limit is reached.

Where data is stored

StepGate stores data locally on your device. We do not send usage or step data to our servers.

Sharing with third parties

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

Third-party services

StepGate does not use third-party analytics or crash reporting services at this time.

Data retention

Data stays on your device until you delete the app data or uninstall StepGate.

How to delete your data

You can remove StepGate’s data by clearing the app’s storage in system settings or by uninstalling the app.

Children’s privacy

StepGate is not directed to children under 13. If you are under the age required in your country to use the app independently, use it with a parent or legal guardian.
