Privacy Policy

This policy explains how StepGate handles your data.

Last updated: 2026-05-22

Product model

StepGate is a paid Android app. It has no ads, no subscriptions, and no in-app store.

Introduction

StepGate helps limit access to selected apps and restore access through physical activity, mainly steps.

Data accessed

  • App usage data for apps you choose to control.
  • Selected app list stored locally to apply limits.
  • Steps from the phone step sensor.
  • Steps from Health Connect, only if you connect it for external step history.
  • Permission states required for app features.

Permissions used

  • Usage Access — to detect the current app and track time in selected apps.
  • Display over other apps — to show the blocking screen over selected apps when access runs out.
  • Activity Recognition — to count steps with the phone sensor.
  • Health Connect — optional external step history, if you enable it.
  • Notifications / foreground service — to keep protection visible and running where Android allows it.
  • Battery optimization / autostart settings — optional system settings that help StepGate keep protection active in the background.

How data is used

  • Calculate the access level for selected apps.
  • Update daily access after new steps.
  • Show a blocking overlay when access runs out.
  • Help you adjust goals and app access locally on the device.

Storage and sharing

Data is stored locally on your device. StepGate does not send app usage data, selected apps, step data, or Health Connect data to StepGate servers.

StepGate does not use ads, trackers, or analytics SDKs.

Legal pages are opened in your external browser.

Health and medical disclaimer

StepGate is not a medical app and does not provide medical advice. Steps are used only to calculate access to selected apps.

What StepGate does not read

  • Messages, chats, or email contents
  • On-screen text in other apps
  • Keyboard input or passwords
  • Photos, files, or contacts
