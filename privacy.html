Privacy Policy

This policy explains how StepGate handles your data.

Last updated: 2026-03-31

Who we are

StepGate is a mobile app that helps you limit access to selected apps and regain access through movement.

What data the app accesses

  • App usage data (usage duration and selected apps) to calculate your baseline time and show insights.
  • Steps / activity data to calculate your daily goal and adjust your available time.
  • Accessibility and usage access (if enabled) to enforce app blocking and confirm access events.

Why we use this data

  • To compute your baseline time from recent usage history.
  • To compute your daily step goal based on your typical activity.
  • To determine the available time for controlled apps each day.
  • To enforce blocking when the daily access limit is reached.

Where data is stored

StepGate stores data locally on your device. We do not send your usage or steps data to our servers.

Sharing with third parties

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. If third-party analytics or crash reporting are added in the future, this policy will be updated.

Permissions used

  • Usage Access — to read app usage for selected apps.
  • Accessibility Service — to enforce app blocking.
  • Activity Recognition / Health Connect — to read steps.
  • Overlay permission — to show the block screen.

Data retention and deletion

All data is stored locally on your device. You can clear the app data or uninstall StepGate to remove it.

Children's privacy

StepGate is not intended for children under the age required by your local laws. If you are a parent or guardian and believe a child has used StepGate, you can remove the app from their device.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at: support@stepgate.app
