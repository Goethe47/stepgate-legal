Privacy Policy

This policy explains how StepGate handles your data.

Last updated: 2026-04-15

Introduction

StepGate is a mobile app that helps you limit access to selected apps and regain access through movement. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is used.

What data StepGate accesses

  • App usage data such as usage duration for selected controlled apps.
  • Steps / activity data from Health Connect and/or supported on-device sources to calculate adaptive access.
  • Permission states to determine whether required features can run.

Permissions used

  • Usage Access — read app usage for selected apps.
  • Accessibility Service — detect launch of selected controlled apps and show the limit screen.
  • Activity Recognition / Health Connect — read step data.
  • Overlay permission — show the limit screen on top of controlled apps (when enabled).

How the data is used

  • Calculate baseline access from recent usage history.
  • Adjust daily access after new step data arrives.
  • Determine available time for controlled apps.
  • Show a limit screen when a controlled app reaches its daily limit.

Where data is stored

StepGate stores data locally on your device. We do not send usage or step data to our servers.

Data may still be processed by core Android platform components that provide permissions and system APIs required for app functionality.

Sharing with third parties

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

Third-party services

StepGate does not use third-party analytics or ad SDKs at this time.

What StepGate does not read

  • Messages, chats, or email contents
  • On-screen text in other apps
  • Keyboard input or passwords
  • Photos, files, or contacts

Accessibility-specific disclosure

Accessibility is used only to detect launch of selected controlled apps and show a limit screen when access is exhausted.

StepGate does not use Accessibility to read messages, extract text, capture keystrokes, or perform unrelated automated actions.

Health Connect rationale

When Health Connect is enabled, StepGate requests read access to step data only. This is used only to update step-based access logic in the app.

Data retention

Data stays on your device until you delete the app data or uninstall StepGate.

How to delete your data

You can remove StepGate’s data by clearing the app’s storage in system settings or by uninstalling the app.

Children’s privacy

StepGate is not directed to children under 13. If you are under the age required in your country to use the app independently, use it with a parent or legal guardian.
