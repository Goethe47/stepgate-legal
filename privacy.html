Privacy Policy

This policy explains how StepGate handles your data.

Last updated: 2026-05-10

Introduction

StepGate helps limit access to selected apps and restore access through movement.

Data accessed

  • App usage data for selected controlled apps.
  • Steps from the phone step sensor.
  • Steps from Health Connect, only if you connect it yourself for external step history.
  • Permission states required for app features.

Permissions used

  • Usage Access — to track time in selected apps.
  • Display over other apps — to show the limit screen over selected apps.
  • Activity Recognition — to count steps with the phone sensor.
  • Health Connect — optional external step history, if you enable it.

How data is used

  • Calculate suggested full access for selected apps.
  • Update daily access after new steps.
  • Determine available time for controlled apps.
  • Show a limit screen when access runs out.

Display over apps disclosure

StepGate uses display-over-apps permission only to show the limit screen over selected controlled apps after access runs out.

StepGate does not use this permission to read messages, extract text, capture keystrokes, or access app content.

Phone steps and Health Connect

StepGate can count steps with the phone sensor when Activity Recognition is granted.

Health Connect is optional. If you connect it, StepGate reads step data only for external step history.

Storage and sharing

Data is stored locally on your device. StepGate does not send usage or step data to our servers.

We do not sell or share personal data with third parties.

What StepGate does not read

  • Messages, chats, or email contents
  • On-screen text in other apps
  • Keyboard input or passwords
  • Photos, files, or contacts
