How We Use Data

A plain-language explanation of why StepGate needs certain permissions.

Last updated: 2026-03-31

Why StepGate needs app usage access

StepGate reads usage data only to understand how much time is spent in the apps you choose to control. This lets us calculate your baseline time and enforce daily limits.

Why StepGate needs step/activity data

Step data helps StepGate adjust your available time based on your movement. More movement means more of your usual app time becomes available.

Why accessibility is needed

Accessibility is used to detect when a controlled app is opened and to show the block screen when your limit is reached. It is not used to read your messages or content.

Does my data leave the device?

No. StepGate stores this data locally on your device and does not send it to our servers.

What if I do not grant a permission?

If a permission is not granted, StepGate will still run but some features may not work. For example, without step data we cannot adjust access based on movement.
