How We Use Data

Plain-language explanation of why StepGate needs certain permissions.

Last updated: 2026-04-16

Why app usage access is needed

StepGate reads usage data only to track time in controlled apps and detect when their limit is exhausted.

Why step/activity data is needed

Step data helps update available access after new records arrive. Data can appear with delay due to source and Health Connect sync behavior.

Why accessibility is needed

Accessibility is used only to detect opening of controlled apps and show the limit screen. It is not used to read messages, screen text, keyboard input, or app content.

Does data leave the device?

No. StepGate stores this data locally and does not send usage/step data to servers.

What if permission is not granted?

StepGate still runs, but related features are limited. For example, without step data, step-based access updates are unavailable.

What StepGate does not access

  • Messages or chat contents
  • On-screen text in other apps
  • Keyboard input or passwords
  • Photos, files, or contacts
